About Syrian Compass

Discover the beauty and rich heritage of Syria through our AI-powered travel recommendation platform.

Our Mission

Syrian Compass is dedicated to promoting the rich cultural heritage and natural beauty of Syria. Our mission is to help travelers discover the perfect Syrian destinations based on their preferences and interests.

Through our AI-powered recommendation system, we aim to provide personalized travel suggestions that match each user's unique preferences, making travel planning easier and more enjoyable.

We believe in the power of tourism to foster cultural understanding, support local communities, and preserve historical sites. By connecting travelers with Syrian cities, we hope to contribute to the appreciation and preservation of Syria's remarkable heritage.

Our Technology

AI-Powered Recommendations

Syrian Compass leverages advanced artificial intelligence to provide personalized travel recommendations. Our system uses the ChatGPT API to understand your preferences through natural conversation.

The AI analyzes various factors including:

  • Your preferred travel season
  • Budget constraints
  • Personal interests (historical sites, beaches, cuisine, etc.)
  • Travel companions (solo, couple, family, friends)
  • Visit objectives (leisure, adventure, cultural immersion, etc.)

Based on this information, our AI recommends the Syrian cities that best match your preferences, along with specific attractions and experiences you might enjoy.

About Syria

Syria is one of the oldest civilizations in the world, with a history dating back thousands of years. It has been home to numerous civilizations, including the Sumerians, Phoenicians, Assyrians, Babylonians, Persians, Greeks, Romans, Byzantines, and various Islamic dynasties.

This rich historical tapestry has left Syria with an incredible wealth of archaeological sites, historical monuments, and cultural traditions. From the ancient ruins of Palmyra to the medieval citadel of Aleppo, Syria's heritage sites offer glimpses into the country's fascinating past.

Beyond its historical significance, Syria boasts diverse landscapes, from Mediterranean beaches to desert oases, mountain ranges, and fertile valleys. The country's cuisine is renowned worldwide for its rich flavors and diverse dishes.

Syrian people are known for their hospitality, warmth, and resilience. Despite the challenges the country has faced, the spirit of the Syrian people and the beauty of their homeland continue to inspire visitors from around the world.

University Project

Syrian Compass is a university project developed as part of a course on web development and artificial intelligence integration. The project aims to demonstrate the practical application of AI in the tourism industry while promoting cultural heritage.
